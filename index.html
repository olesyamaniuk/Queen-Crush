About Us

Lollipop Sweet Heroes Match3 is a delightful match-3 puzzle game that brings you into a world of colorful candies and sweet adventures. Match tiles, unleash powerful boosters, and team up with candy heroes to conquer hundreds of exciting levels. Whether you're a casual player or a puzzle enthusiast, Lollipop Sweet Heroes Match3 offers an engaging and visually stunning gaming experience that’s as sweet as it is challenging.

    Boosters are special tools that help you clear levels faster and overcome challenging puzzles.
