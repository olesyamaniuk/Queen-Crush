About the Game

Queen Crush is a royal-themed match-3 puzzle game where you journey through an enchanted kingdom filled with dazzling jewels, magical surprises, and endless fun. Swap and match gems to complete thrilling levels, collect rewards, and unlock new areas of the queen's majestic realm.

With each victory, you'll help the queen restore her kingdom’s glory, uncover hidden treasures, and celebrate in dazzling royal style. From sparkling castles to mythical lands, every step of your journey is packed with excitement, charm, and sparkling gameplay.

FAQ

Is Queen Crush free to play?
Yes, Queen Crush is free to play with optional in-app purchases for boosters, lives, and premium features.
Can I play offline?
Yes, you can enjoy the game offline, but some features like events and leaderboards require an internet connection.
What should I do if I run out of lives?
Lives regenerate over time. You can also ask friends for lives or purchase them from the store.
Are there any time-limited events?
Absolutely! Queen Crush features seasonal and themed events that offer exclusive rewards and unique gameplay elements.
How often are new levels added?
New levels are added regularly through updates, ensuring fresh challenges and adventures.
Can I sync my progress across devices?
Yes, by connecting to your social account, you can save and sync your progress across multiple devices.
